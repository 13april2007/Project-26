Chocolate Cake

Ingredients-
1. flour
2. sugar
3. milkmaid
4. Chocolate
5. BUtter
6. Biscuits
7. Baking Powder
8. Milk
9. choco chip

Recipe-
1. Add sugar, milkmaid, flour, baking Powder and butter in a bowl
2. mix them all while adding milk time to time
3. bake it for 45mins in an oven
4. Let it cool down and sprinkle the choco chips
